ABOUT US

ABOUT US



Creation is involved in manufacturing and exporting an exclusive range of Leather Bags & Accessories like Leather Wallets and Designer Leather Bags, Portfolio Bags, Travel Bags and many more designs. These are developed using finest leather, which is quality tested on well defined parameters like tensile strength, resistance to tear, resistance to puncture, resistance to flex and heat conductivity. This helps to ensure that the end product is completely flawless and has a longer shelf life. We export our products in Europe, United States, Japan, Australia, etc.

Our sophisticated manufacturing unit is based at Kolkata, which is equipped with modern machines that ensure fast and effective production procedures. All these facilities help us to offer an extensive range of innovative products and the ability to customize our range as per the specific demands of our clients. Owing to our quality range of products, customer centric approach and wide distribution network we have been able to spread our horizons in various parts of the globe.

Mr. Arunabha Dutta,Proprietor and Founder of Creation, holds an affluent industry experience in the respective domain. He plays a vital role in guiding our team and has assisted in acquiring respectable position in the global arena.

We offer quality products to our clients. Our products are created by our designers who carefully work on even small areas and for complete look and style. We consistently endeavor to reach the unmatched quality standards in our range of Leather Bags and Accessories and ensure that these are designed in compliance with the set industry norms. Our team of quality controllers ensures that every stage of our manufacturing process undergoes strict vigilance right from the initial inspection of incoming material to the final dispatch of the consignment. Manufacturing Facilities

Our organization has set up a state-of-the-art manufacturing unit, which is spread across a sprawling area with an appreciable installed capacity. The unit is completely equipped with all requisite facilities including latest designing software. This helps to manufacture our range of Leather Bags and Accessories in compliance with the international quality standards. Further, with the use modern machines and tools we have been able to complete the bulk orders in an efficient manner and deliver the consignment within the stipulated time frame.

Warehousing and Packaging

We have set up an organized warehousing unit to store our bulk range of Leather Bags and Accessories in secured manner. The unit is kept moisture free so that our leather products remain in a proper shape. Thus, we have segregated our warehouse into different units to ensure timely and hassle free delivery.

Apart from this, we also take due care in suitably packaging our leather products using quality packaging material like gated boxes, cartons and sheets helps to ensure safe and sound supply. These packaging are available in varied weights and capacities with proper labeling and information for our clients' convenience.
